01About JPFR

A multidisciplinary practice at the intersection of care, creativity and technology.

JPFR is the authorial and professional identity of Juan Pablo Fernández Rebosio, an Argentine–Italian psychiatrist and founder of Landia’s Productions.

His work explores how ethical technology, mental-health knowledge and audiovisual culture can help communities document responsibly, communicate safely, recover from trauma and collaborate across borders.
